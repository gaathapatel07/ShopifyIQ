#  ShopifyIQ

### Transforming E-Commerce Data into Actionable Business Intelligence

ShopifyIQ is a modern E-Commerce Analytics Platform that empowers businesses to make data-driven decisions through interactive dashboards, KPI monitoring, customer analytics, sales insights, and inventory intelligence.

Built using React, TypeScript, Firebase, Tailwind CSS, and Recharts, ShopifyIQ transforms raw business data into meaningful visualizations and actionable insights.

##  Overview

E-commerce businesses generate massive amounts of data every day, but extracting meaningful insights from this data remains a challenge.

ShopifyIQ helps businesses:

- Monitor sales performance
- Track customer behavior
- Analyze inventory health
- Measure business KPIs
- Discover revenue opportunities
- Make smarter business decisions

---

##  Problem Statement

Many businesses struggle with:

- Lack of centralized analytics
- Poor visibility into sales trends
- Customer retention challenges
- Inventory management inefficiencies
- Limited business intelligence capabilities

ShopifyIQ addresses these challenges by providing an intuitive analytics dashboard that converts data into actionable insights.
